The Dwarkadhish Temple in Naini Village, Chhapra is built on the lines of the Dwarkadheesh Temple in Gujarat . This temple has been completed in 14 years, in which artisans from Gujarat have worked. Dhagadhra stone has been used in the construction of the temple, which has been brought from Surendranagar in Gujarat.

About

The Dwarkadhish Temple in Naini, Chhapra, is built without using bricks, cement, iron rods, or sand. This temple is constructed from expensive marble stones, which make it look very attractive. People from far and wide come to this temple to have a glimpse of Lord Krishna. The temple has also provided employment opportunities to the local residents
